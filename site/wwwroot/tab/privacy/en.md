title: Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

One More Cap is designed to work locally on your Mac. It does not require an account, advertising identifier, analytics SDK, or tracking SDK.

## Data collection

One More Cap does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not upload your subtitle files, movie files, playback history, or caption text to One More Cap servers.

## Local files

When you choose or drag a subtitle file into the app, One More Cap reads that user-selected file on your Mac so it can display captions. Supported subtitle formats are SRT and WebVTT.

## Playback sync

When you press Sync, One More Cap may ask macOS for Automation permission so it can read QuickTime Player's current playback position. This is used only to align subtitle timing.

The App Store build syncs with QuickTime Player only. It does not include Apple TV Accessibility sync or Sparkle update checking.

The GitHub build may include optional Apple TV sync through Accessibility and optional update checks through Sparkle. Those features are not part of the App Store build.

## Network use

The App Store build does not need a One More Cap account or One More Cap server to display subtitles or sync with QuickTime Player.

Links opened from the website, GitHub releases, or support pages are handled by your browser.
